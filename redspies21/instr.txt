*********************Game rules***********************************
Red and Blue team moves one by one (reds first).

Both teams have 6 standard units ("soldiers") and 2 spy units:
reds - 6xR && S1/S2 ('S' means "Stirlitz");
blues - 6xB && E1/E2 ('E' means "Emissarius").

Step width for soldier unit is 1, while spies have basic (1 step) and
super (2 steps) moves. Spy units have limit for "Super" jump.

Soldier can attack unit only in his line "face to face" (like pawn,or
"peshka", in chess), while Spy unit cannot attack in front, but can 
"sneaky attack"("in spine" (behind in same line), or in diagonal 
(up-right,up-left,down-left,down-right). For both units must exists 
empty space segment backward enemy unit (like in checkers).

The 2 goals of each team for victory is:

1) 4 figure must get to the last field cell (7 row for Reds and 0 for
Blues);

2) or eliminate half of enemy team units ("surrender").
